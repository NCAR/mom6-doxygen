program obs_sampling_err

Looking at sampling error for the obs. space part of the problem Assume that the ratio of the prior sample to obs standard dev. is known Want to compute the following:

  1. An unbiased estimate of the updated variance
  2. An unbiased estimate of the updated mean which should be expressed as the fraction of the distance to move between the prior sample mean and the observed value
  3. The standard deviation or variance of the updated mean estimate Need to add this uncertainty into the sample to avoid loss of variance (see also full_error.f90 which does this for regression).
